Future of Work

How AI is transforming roles, skills, and organisations — and what UX researchers need to understand to study this shift.

Mini Playbook: Future of Work Research

🕐 When to Use This Knowledge

  • Researching enterprise AI adoption and worker attitudes
  • Studying how professionals adapt to AI-assisted work
  • Understanding comparative advantage in human vs AI tasks
  • Exploring job displacement anxieties in user research
  • Designing studies on AI productivity and skill development

🔑 Key Considerations

  • AI exposure varies enormously by job type and seniority
  • Comparative advantage means humans may still do some AI-capable tasks
  • Worker anxiety about AI is nuanced — not just fear of job loss
  • Enterprise adoption is slower than individual adoption — chaos matters
  • The "jagged frontier" means skills that matter change unpredictably

⚠️ Common Mistakes

  • Assuming AI adoption is uniform across roles and industries
  • Conflating "AI can do this task" with "AI will replace this job"
  • Ignoring the messy, political reality of enterprise change management
  • Not distinguishing between senior and junior employee AI experiences
  • Treating productivity gains as automatically equating to job losses
