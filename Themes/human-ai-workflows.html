Human + AI Workflows

How to work effectively alongside AI — mental models, practical strategies, and the right division of labour for researchers.

Mini Playbook: Human + AI Collaboration

🕐 When to Use This Knowledge

  • Designing AI-assisted research workflows
  • Onboarding a team to AI research tools
  • Evaluating which research tasks to delegate to AI
  • Developing team norms around AI use in synthesis
  • Managing the "centaur" model in collaborative research

🔑 Key Considerations

  • The Centaur model (human directs AI) outperforms solo AI or solo human
  • AI excels at breadth; humans add value at depth and judgment
  • Cognitive offloading is a real risk — protect human analytical skill
  • Define AI-off zones in your research process intentionally
  • Memorisation still matters — don't outsource all thinking to AI

⚠️ Common Mistakes

  • Using AI for every step, losing deep engagement with the data
  • Not validating AI outputs against source material
  • Letting AI choose the direction — always keep humans in the driver's seat
  • Treating "AI did it" as sufficient for stakeholder credibility
  • Ignoring team knowledge-sharing loss from individual AI querying
